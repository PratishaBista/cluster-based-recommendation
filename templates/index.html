MUSIC RECOMMENDER

I. Introduction

This experimentation involving the application of unsupervised machine learning techniques to the field of music recommendation is an attempt to understand the mechanics of music discovery. The central question behind this project is: "How can one discover songs that share a similar musical vibe?" Instead of relying on popularity or user interaction data, the focus is placed on identifying similarity through measurable audio properties such as valence, danceability, acousticness, energy, etc.

II. Methodology: K-Means Clustering

The system operates in two distinct phases:

  1. Clustering: Songs are grouped into high-dimensional clusters based on audio properties.
  2. Vector Quantization: Once a target song is selected, the engine calculates the Cosine Similarity between the target and other tracks within the same cluster to find the closest matches.

V. Glossary of Features

Valence
A measure from 0.0 to 1.0 describing the musical positiveness. High valence sounds cheerful.
Danceability
How suitable a track is for dancing based on tempo, rhythm stability, beat strength, etc.
Acousticness
A confidence measure of whether the track is acoustic.
Energy
A perceptual measure of intensity and activity (loud, fast, noisy).
